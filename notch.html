Notch Therapy

Removes energy at your tinnitus frequency from broadband noise, encouraging cortical reorganization (TMNMT).

Clinical Significance

Per Okamoto et al. (2010), removing energy at the tinnitus pitch triggers lateral inhibition, which aims to reduce perceived loudness via cortical reorganization over 3–12 months of consistent use.
