Recommended Use

Guidelines for safe and effective sound therapy.

Best Listening Setup

  • Use earbuds or over‑ear headphones
  • Keep volume at a comfortable level
  • Use in a quiet environment

Therapy Guidelines

  • Decorrelated noise: 20–30 minutes daily
  • Notch therapy: Use your matched frequency
  • Sound therapy: Use for relaxation or masking

Safety

  • Stop immediately if you feel discomfort
  • Do not exceed safe listening levels
  • This is not a medical device
