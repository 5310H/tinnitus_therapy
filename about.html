About the Project

An open-source initiative for accessible auditory research.

Our Mission

The Trahreg Tinnitus Therapy Suite was created to bridge the gap between clinical research and accessible technology. Many effective sound therapy protocols are locked behind expensive proprietary devices; our goal is to provide these tools for free, with total privacy, to anyone with a web browser.

  • Privacy First: No tracking, no cookies, and no data collection. Your hearing data stays on your device.
  • Research-Led: Every tool is built following parameters established in peer-reviewed clinical trials.
  • Offline Capability: Designed to work anywhere, ensuring therapy is available without an active internet connection.

Technology Stack

The suite is built using modern, lightweight web technologies to ensure maximum portability and performance:

  • 🛠 Web Audio API: For high-precision, real-time audio synthesis and filtering.
  • 💾 Local Storage: For secure, local-only persistence of therapy settings.
  • 🐍 Python (NumPy/SciPy): Powering the offline calibrated WAV generator.
  • 📄 Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS: No heavy frameworks, ensuring long-term maintainability.

Clinical Specifications

  • 🎯 Stereo Calibration: Fully independent L/R frequency and volume controls to support asymmetric tinnitus.
  • 📉 Filter Precision: Notch filters validated at >40dB attenuation with ±1% center frequency accuracy.
  • 🔄 Neuromodulation: Independent channel modulation and tone bursting following TENT-A timing protocols.
  • 🎧 Phase Logic: Integrated hardware phase auditing to ensure correct signal delivery for decorrelated noise.
  • 🛡 System Health: Automated 30-day validation cycle to ensure audio engine integrity.

Professional Review

To facilitate professional medical review, the suite allows you to share your configuration and usage data directly with your clinician:

  • Configure Email: On the Home page, expand the Project card and enter your Audiologist's email address. This setting is saved locally on your device.
  • Send Reports: Within any therapy module, click the Email to Audiologist button.
  • Clinical Context: This generates a pre-formatted email containing your specific therapy settings, daily usage logs, and the results of your hardware and system validation tests for your doctor to review.
